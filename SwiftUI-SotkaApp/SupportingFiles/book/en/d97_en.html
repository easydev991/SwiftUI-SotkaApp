Day 97. FREESTYLE complex Reverse Horse Move




Today you are going to see the last of the freestyle complex consisted of different types of pushups, so let's not waste time!

Not many people know why this complex is called exactly like that, but that quesition should be addressed to Alexander Prokhorov, member of The Patriots team, who invented it.

The complex consists of 5 sets, in each set you do 5 types of push-ups in a row, one by one, without rest:
decline push-ups,
regular push-ups from the floor,
incline push-ups,
regular push-ups from the floor,
decline push-ups.

Depending on how well you are prepared for the training in each type of push-ups you do: 5 repetitions and rest for 1 minute between the sets if you are a beginner, 7 reps and rest for 1-2 minutes between the set if you are advanced level or 10 repetitions and rest for 2-3 minutes between the set if you eager to face that challenge!
