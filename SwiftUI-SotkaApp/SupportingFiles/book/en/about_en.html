О программе




SOTKA program was developed as an educational course for beginners, who just start their training or get back to the training regime after a long brake or injury. SOTKA was created by Anton Kuchumov, one of the founders of Street Workout.

The program is based around bodyweight exercises combined in circuits of 3-4 exercises and performed without rest in between. Additionally, each participant receives on a daily basis an informational block with answers on all the questions regarding training, dieting, self development, time management, fitness in general and other topics. We want to make people aware of the benefits of regular physical activity and provide them with necessary knowledge for further development.
