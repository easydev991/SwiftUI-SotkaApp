Day 94. TURBO complex BETA




We hope that you've liked our new training approach, so let's continue!

After the warm-up we the horseman exercise, that is already familiar to you, for 30-60 seconds, and move on to the main complex!

The following 3 exercises are done one after another (that is 1 circuit) for 5 cirtuits straight without rest between them!

1.Hindu push-ups - according to the name, this exercise has come from India. You can see the technique for this exercise in the video below:



Do 5-10 repetitions.

2. Spartan squats – quite an interesting exercise, which would be better to perform (at least at first) standing on one leg and holding the bar with two arms. You squat by bending one leg, while the second one (already bent in knee) we move behind body as we move down. Don’t forget to keep correct body posture even when you lean forward. 5-10 times for each leg.



3. Tuck L-sit pull-ups -frankly speaking, this particular kind of pull-ups would be probably hard for you, because you don't have enought muscle coordination. Still worth trying!



Bend knees and keep 90 degrees angle between your legs and your abs, or you can lift your legs to your chest, or you can keep your legs straight with 90 degrees angle between your legs and your abs (if you are real master of the bar). 5-10 reps without swinging!
