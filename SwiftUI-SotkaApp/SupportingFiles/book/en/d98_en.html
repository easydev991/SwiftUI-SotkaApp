Day 98. TURBO complex DELTA




Welcome to the last TURBO complex!

Today you should warm up properly, because we will do “ladders”. Those, who aren’t new to street workout or remember their childhood years spent outdoors, know what I mean. Here is a short instruction for everyone else..

There is no starting exercise this time, although you can include any exercise we mentioned earlier into your warm-up. Let’s get down to today’s routine – a set of three exercises. You will do it only once with a full recovery after each exercise (5-7 minutes, you should recover, but not “cool down”). And, as always, keep in mind the proper technique!

1. Pull-ups. Do 1, get down, do 2, get down, do 3 and so on until you reach your maximum. Then start a descending ladder, i.e. if your maximum was 10, then you do 9, 8, 7 etc. If you can’t do 7 in one attempt, split it into several (each should be maximised!), the main goal is to do the necessary number. And you move towards 1. No pauses between attempts - you jump down from the bar, shake your arms and get back up!



2. Push-ups. You do push-ups following the same pattern, but this time you add (and remove) 2 repetitions instead of 1. I.e. You do 2 push-ups, get up, do 4, get up and so on. Again, no pauses between attempts.



Squats. The pattern is the same, but the number of reps for each step is 5-10 (depending on your abilities). I.e. you do 10, stand up, shake up, do 20, stand up, shake up and so on. No pauses between attempts.



If you truly do everything to the maximum, you will be incapable of doing anything else, but you won't need to! This is the FINAL routine of our training course, and you deserve a round of applause after finishing it. And tomorrow we will rest and recap everything.
