Day 93. FREESTYLE complex BAR




This block is called TURBO FREESTYLE for a reason, because every day we will alternate TURBO and FREESTLYLE training sessions. Today you are going to complete your FIRST freestyle complex. First, do a warm-up and a horseman exercise for 30-60 seconds.

As in all FREESTYLE complexes today you are going to do 5 sets, which will take your soul out of you body and make you curse the whole world. But if you survive, then you will leave the tplayround full of grace and appeasement! You should try to do all the sets without getting off the bar. But if you feel exhausted and slip out, then take a small rest and go on. Rest between sets is 3-5 minutes depending on your feelings.

1 set:
Х pull-ups in the lower part of the exercise (from a dead hang to an angle of 90 degrees in elbows)
Х pull-ups in the higher part of the exercise (from 90 degrees in elbows to the chin above the bar)
X full pull-ups

X = 3 if you are a beginner, X = 5 if you are advanced and X = 7 if you are master of the bar.

2 set:
In this set you start with 3 narrow grip pull-ups, and after every 3 reps you distant palms from each other by the width of the two palms. It means that every 3 repetitions your grip will become wider and wider. Go for the maximum repetitions, until you fall or until the width of the bar ends.

3 set:
Х pull-ups – Y secs hold with chin above the bar,
Х pull-ups – Y secs hold with 90 degrees angle in elbows,
Х pull-ups – Y secs dead hang,
Х pull-ups – Y secs hold with 90 degrees angle in elbows,
Х pull-ups – Y secs hold with chin above the bar.

X = 2 and Y = 5 if you are a beginner, X = 3 and Y = 7 if you are advanced and X = 4 and Y = 7 if you are master of the bar.

4 set:
In this set, you start with 3 pull-ups with the usual upper grip, then move one of the hands into the lower grip and do 3 more pull-ups (with mixed grip), then move the second hand to the lower grip and do 3 more chin-ups (lower grip), then again the first hand moves to the upper grip and you do 3 pull-ups more (with mixed grip), then move the second hand and do 3 pull-ups more with the upper grip. If you want, you can do this set one more time.

5 set:
Do maximum pull-ups with neutral grip on one bar.
