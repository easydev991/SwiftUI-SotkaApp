Day 96. TURBO complex GAMMA




We keep on going in TURBO mode! And today, after a good warm-up, we do our favourite horseman exercuse (1 time) and then proceed to 5 circuits of the following exercises (no rest between exercises, rest between circtuits is up to you)!

1. Slow push-ups. You can do them either in tempo 5-0-5 or 5-0-1 (with explosive push). Number of repetitions - 5-10.



2. Slow squats. The same idea as in previous exercise, same tempo 5-0-5 or 5-0-1. Number of repetitions - 10-20.

3. Slow pull-ups. Again, choose either tempo 5-0-5 or 5-0-1 and do 2-5 repetitions.

Good luck, have fun, and tomorrow you will have new challenge!
